Melbourne Ruby

When

Melbourne Ruby meetings happen on the last Thursday of every month (with the exception of December which has a social event sometime in the middle of the month).

Our next meeting is Thursday 28th June 2012.

Where

Inspire9
Level 1, 41-43 Stewart St
Richmond
6:00pm for a 6:30pm start!

Organisers

Justin French (@justinfrench).

Format

  • News & events
  • Who's hiring, who's available
  • Lightning talks, tutorials and presentations on Ruby and related topics
  • Dinner and drinks at the Corner Hotel

Get Involved

Chatter about upcoming meetings takes place on the Ruby or Rails Oceania Google Group.

Presenters, organisers and sponsors are always welcome.
